Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025
Heads up: This policy explains how Taylor Real Estate Group LLC (“we”, “us”) and our operating brand Prime Land Solutions handle your information across our websites (including pgtaylorrei.com), forms, calls/SMS, and services.

1) Information We Collect

a) Information you provide to us

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number, mailing address)
  • Property information (site address, parcel/lot details, zoning, acreage, photos you share)
  • Inquiry details (messages, meeting notes, call summaries)
  • Transaction details (offers, contracts, invoices, payment confirmations)
  • Preferences (communication preferences, marketing opt-ins)

b) Information we collect automatically

  • Device and usage data (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, timestamps)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see §7 Cookies & Tracking)
  • Location information (approximate location based on IP)

c) Information from third parties

  • Lead sources and data partners (public records, property databases, skip-trace providers)
  • Analytics/ads partners (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta/Facebook Pixel)
  • Service providers (CRM, forms, scheduling, payment processors)

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information unless you choose to provide it and we have a lawful basis to process it.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • Respond to inquiries, schedule site visits, evaluate properties, and deliver proposals
  • Perform and manage contracts, purchases, sales, and dirt-work jobs
  • Send SMS and email updates you request or consent to (e.g., property opportunities, job updates, reminders)
  • Personalize content and ads; measure and improve advertising performance
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security risks
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

AI & Call Processing. We may use AI tools (e.g., voice agents/transcription) to assist with scheduling, note-taking, and customer support. Where permitted by law, we may record or transcribe calls for quality and training. See §10 for your choices.

3) Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or UK, we process personal data under one or more of the following legal bases: (i) performance of a contract; (ii) legitimate interests; (iii) consent; and (iv) legal obligations.

4) Sharing of Information

  • Vendors/Service Providers (hosting, CRM, analytics, payment, communications, document signing)
  • Professional partners (title/escrow, attorneys, engineers, surveyors, contractors, lenders) as needed for transactions/jobs you request
  • Advertising/Analytics partners (see §7)
  • Business transfers (merger, sale, financing, or acquisition)
  • Legal and safety (comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud/abuse)

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense. We may engage in cross-context behavioral advertising/“targeted advertising”; see §9 for opt-out rights where applicable.

5) Data Retention

We retain information only as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6) Your Choices

  • Emails: Unsubscribe anytime using the link in our emails.
  • SMS/Text Messages: Reply STOP to opt out; HELP for help. Message/data rates may apply. Frequency varies.
  • Cookies: Manage preferences via your browser or our cookie banner (where available).
  • Analytics/Ads: See §7 and §9 for opt-out options.

7) Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We and our partners use cookies, pixels, and similar tools to provide and improve the Services and for analytics and advertising. Examples include essential cookies, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and advertising pixels (e.g., Meta/Facebook Pixel, Google Ads). You may disable cookies or use industry opt-outs (see §9). Some features may not function properly without cookies.

8) Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9) Privacy Rights (U.S. State Laws)

Depending on your state (e.g., Tennessee, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia), you may have rights to know/access, correct, delete, and opt out of “sale”/targeted advertising/profiling. Tennessee (TIPA) & California (CCPA/CPRA) residents may exercise these rights by contacting us (see §12). We will verify your request and respond as required by law.

Industry opt-out tools: Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out; Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) opt-out; YourAdChoices/AppChoices.

10) Communications, Recording & AI Tools

  • Texts/SMS: By providing your number, you consent to receive SMS from us. Opt out anytime by replying STOP.
  • Calls/Voicemail: We may record calls for quality and training where permitted by law. We will provide notice where required. If you prefer not to be recorded, please tell us and we will provide alternatives.
  • AI Assistants/Transcription: We may use AI services to transcribe/route messages and assist with scheduling, lead intake, and customer support.

11) Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
